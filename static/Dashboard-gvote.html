GVote - Online Voting Website

Instructions

Welcome! to GVote. Start casting your votes by selecting SFS or GAS from the above options (make sure to select your correct stream). Different categories of the particular stream will be shown. Choose a category, cast your vote for your favourite candidate. Likewise, cast votes for all the categories available and click the submit button, once you have voted for all the categories. Keep in mind that once you have clicked the submit button, you cannot make any changes. After your successful vote casting, you can log out from the website.

"Somewhere inside of all of us is the power to make a change, so start your voting."
Happy Voting!!!
